About

My project, the South African Car Weather App, was born out of a deep-seated need for a reliable weather application tailored to the specific challenges faced by car owners in South Africa. As a sole developer, I recognized the critical importance of staying informed about weather conditions, particularly when driving alone.

One of the key insights driving the development of this app was the recognition of how seamlessly integrating a calendar feature could enhance its utility. By incorporating a comprehensive calendar function, users can not only access real-time weather updates but also plan their journeys effectively, aligning them with their scheduled appointments and travel plans.
